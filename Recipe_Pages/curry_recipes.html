Acorn Squash Curry 9/10
Filling
Sweet		 400 Incredibly delicious and sweet curry. I ended up making a lot of alterations to the recipe to get rid of some ingredients I had. I added a left-over zucchini I had, putting it in when I put in the tomatoes. I also used canellini beans instead. I ate this with basmati rice and garnished with cilantro.
Thai Eggplant Curry 9/10 180 The website didn't post the calories, so I used Trader Joe's calories for their version of Thai eggplant curry. This was my first time using lemon grass in a recipe, and I loved it! The texture of these little eggplants is quite exquisite. I used a zester to grate the turmeric and ginger. I bought the lemon grass pre-grated and frozen from an Asian Supermarket. I'm a big fan of Chef Buck's recipe videos.
